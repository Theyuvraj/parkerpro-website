The ParkerPro 5-Phase SEO Methodology

We treat Amazon SEO as a dynamic, ongoing process. Our proprietary methodology provides a clear, measurable journey to dominate organic search rankings.

Ensure your product is properly indexed by Amazon's A9 algorithm for its most critical core terms, based on deep competitor research.
Strategically refine the title and bullet points to incorporate secondary keywords and remove redundant terms from the backend to expand your indexing footprint.
Identify keywords where you rank on pages 2-5 (positions 20-50) and launch a concerted PPC and SEO push to drive them onto the critical first page.
Leverage Amazon's Search Query Performance data to identify and integrate the market's highest-converting search terms directly into your strategy.
